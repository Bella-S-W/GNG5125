Green Transport Assistant

Explore green transport research with a chatbot.

This webpage presents the Green Transport Bot, a Dialogflow-powered assistant that helps users ask questions about sustainable transportation topics such as urban navigation, UAV applications, railway applications, autonomous infrastructure, energy storage, and technical constraints.

Click the chatbot icon in the bottom-right corner to start chatting.
